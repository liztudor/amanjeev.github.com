I also have a B&W version of this picture which has amazing texture of this bench but I guess more folks in the world have better/more cone cells in their eyes than I do. My response to the B&W picture was more positive than this one, even though I love this picture.

This picture was taken in the American University, Washington D.C. Photography can be very unpredictable when it comes to subjects. One can have as many themes and one can keep on shooting the entire day but the best piece of the day may certainly be something you just randomly clicked. For my future self, I need to remember two things -

  1. Click more and more. I am not paying for the film so why not?
  2. Step outside of the subject boundary and not be confined. This will take a lot of effort despite the fact that I know it very well that I have to do it.

Wish me luck because this is a fortuitous profession.
